About Me

I've spent a great deal of time learning various aspects of the creative world over the last decade. I started back in 2012 with audio work including sound production and audio mastering. In 2015 I started learning 3D design and was able to apply it to a great cause in 2017 with work for CNDG (Chang Newall Development Group) developing Visual Learning Environments (VLE's) for college level students through Pearson Publishing. I've also focused on studying various other applications for 3D including 3D-printing, video game development, rendering, property mockups and a variety of other applications. I also have a passion for capturing the world around me and being able to show the beauty in what I see through photography.
